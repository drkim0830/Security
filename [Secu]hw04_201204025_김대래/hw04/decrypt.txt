gimchi is the accepted word in both north and south korean standard languages. earlier forms of the word include timchai, a middle korean tran scription of the sino-korean word. timchai appears in sohak eonhae, the 16th century korean rendition of the chinese book, xiaoxue(in korean, sohak). sound changes from middle korean to modern korean regarding the word can be described as: timchai > dimchai > jimchai > jimchui > gimchi
